Events List


1. Campus Tours

All tours start at the assembly point near the Registration desk.
Academic Tour:
Walk around with our student guides and see the classrooms, research labs, library, IT-center, offices and more…
Campus Life:Let our students show you their favourite hangouts on campus – dining hall, places to snack and chat, activity centres and clubs, gym and sports facilities, hostel rooms.
IIITD v2.0:
We have plans for expansion. Learn how the campus will change in its Phase-II construction. See plans for more classrooms, labs, offices, auditoriums, hostels and a sports complex with squash courts, swimming pool and much more.
Virtual IIITD:Explore our campus while sitting at a computer. Exploring the campus with the created by our students.

2. Info Session on IIITD

You can learn about all that IIIT-Delhi offers – from the degree programmes and placements to entrepreneurship cell and student activities. All sessions will be held by IIITD experts in the respective areas. at C01 (main classroom on ground floor)

3. Visit the Research Labs

What does it mean to do research @IIITD? Visit some of our research labs and interact with research scholars, learn about the area!
Read more

4. Student Projects & Demos

See what our students learn to create. Students will present projects and demos done in courses, at student clubs or simply for fun. Come to C02 and see demos of Online Feedback System, Voice over IP System, MobilEye, IIITD Radio and many more…
A detailed schedule will be displayed on 14th March outside C02 and at the registration desk
