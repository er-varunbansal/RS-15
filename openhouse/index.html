IIIT-Delhi Open House

What is an Open House?

With the Open House IIIT-Delhi invites the general public to see its facilities, to get first-hand information about the programmes offered and the research going on. Visitors may join guided campus tours, attend info sessions and interact with faculty and students over research, studies, campus life etc.

Who are invited?

Everyone. In particular, a visit to the Open House will be interesting for students and their families who consider joining IIIT-D as a BTech, MTech or PhD student, professional and students from other universities curious about the research conducted at IIIT-Delhi, people wanting to know how a modern research-based university is functioning.

Why an Open House?

As an institute supported by the Delhi Government, IIIT-Delhi wants to give the public the opportunity to see 'their' university from inside. The Open House is further meant to assist students and parents in making the right choice when deciding on the educational institute to join.
