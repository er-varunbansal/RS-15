Introduction

Do you have the spark in you that makes you stand out? Then come and showcase your talent at the most happening hardware hackathon in the town. IOT Hackathon is a week long hardware hackathon in RS’15 in which teams come together to make awesome things happen!

Event Details

Initial set of design problems will be released on 1st March, 2015 followed by the beginning of hackathon on 07th March with participants submitting their proposals along with the list of components. The selected proposals will be intimated by 10th March and will be called onsite for a fun filled 24 hour long event. You are allowed to bring in your own components provided they are verified by our team.

Team size

You can come along with your team or may interact with lone wolfs onsite and form a wolfpack! Ideally a team should consist of a hardware guy, the software geek and a tinkerer. But teams are free to make any combination. Maximum team size would be restricted to 4 (four).

Prizes and Judging

The prizes are judged on three categories : Innovation-How innovative and novel your prototype is, what is the Market Scope of your product and the Jury reviews.

NB

IIITD will provide hardware support (Intel Galileo, sensors, arduino Uno) during on the spot IOT hackathon. Please submit your requirements along with your proposal on 7th March. Teams are free to choose their own design problems within the theme of “IOT”.

Incase you have any queries or contact sanchit12156@iiitd.ac.in , manojg@iiitd.ac.in and vibhutesh1494@iiitd.ac.in

Sell your work, and you have two minutes to say it all.

The idea of this event is to showcase creative and groundbreaking ideas which are not yet published but are consolidated in the form a hardware design or a web application or a piece of software. Even if it is under review or not meant for publication still you can present it.

What we are looking for ?

Something innovative which is demonstrable in a concrete form and should be working. It can be a hardware design, software application or a fusion of both. But it should have a strong conceptual idea and impact to the technical or research community in broad.

How this event will happen ?

All the registrations will be filtered on basis of impact and quality of work. The selected teams/individuals will be given 2-minutes [single slide presentation] to advertise their work in front of our reputed technical jury followed by a 2 hour long demo session for audience (including jury members).

Awards ?

The demo which gets maximum user votes will get “User choice award (demo category)” and best innovative demo will be honoured with “Best demo award ( in terms of research contribution)”

Constraints-

Your demo should be having strong technical/ research impact and it should be demonstrable. We won’t encourage pure engineering based work.

Logistics-

Each team/ individual have to mention their specific need like a table, extension cord, poster pinup board during registration. Last minute requirements won’t be catered.
